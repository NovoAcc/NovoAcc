Harborview Farms

A 10,000+ acre farm on Maryland's Eastern Shore, Harborview produces corn, wheat and soybeans. The mission of Harborview Farms includes sustainable agriculture – particularly due to the location on the farm, near the fragile ecosystem of the Chesapeake Bay. Best management practices and integrated pest management are employed to ensure optimum productivity and protection of the environment while contributing to the restoration of the Chesapeake Bay.
