DuPont™ Vamac® AEM elastomer & DuPont™ Nomex® fibers

Today's smaller, more efficient engines deliver more power. But their systems generate more heat and pressure. DuPont™ Vamac® AEM elastomer and DuPont™ Nomex® fibers stand up to demanding engine environments, offering significant performance and cost advantages.
